About Us

Degung Hillside is a new cottage and resto located at Desa Cibedug, just a short drive away from toll gate Ciawi and also accessible via two different routes either to Puncak or to Ciawi-Sukabumi. Surrounded by stunning beauty and vivid presence of Mount Pangrango and Mount Salak, along with the splendid/exceptional view of Ciawi area of the hills.

In this serene environment, Degunghillside is your picture-perfect weekend getaway and outing programs. The countryside cottages & resto will over the perfect blend of scenic vacation & outing activities; meeting, fun outdoor activities & rejuvenating wellness & spiritual programs in a Family, Communities, employees & students.
